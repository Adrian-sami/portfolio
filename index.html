My Background

I began my professional journey in Equatorial Guinea, where I served as a restaurant manager in the hospitality sector. This role gave me a strong foundation in operational management, customer engagement, and the importance of real-time performance monitoring.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I worked as a data analyst with the Ministry of Health of Equatorial Guinea, in collaboration with the World Health Organization (WHO). This experience deepened my understanding of data's power in shaping effective public policies.

Following this, I moved to India, where I worked in airline operations and cruise line services as a sales agent, engaging with international clients across the USA, Canada, and Latin American countries.
